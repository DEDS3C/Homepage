Inside and Outside Control

An assessment has been completed to validate the need for an upgrade to your small- to medium-sized wireless network. Approved for purchase are indoor and outdoor access points and one wireless controller. You must compare equipment models and their specifications before you purchase.

Therefore, you visit the “Wireless Compare Products and Services“ web site and see a features chart for indoor and outdoor wireless access points and controller devices. After reviewing the chart, you note there is some terminology with which you are unfamiliar:

  • Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS)

  • MIMO

  • Cisco CleanAir Technology

  • Cisco FlexConnect

  • Band Select

Research the above terms. Prepare your own chart with your company’s most important requirements listed for purchasing the indoor and outdoor wireless access points and wireless controller. This chart will assist in validating your purchase order to your accounting manager and CEO.
