Step 1, align the CPU with the socket and gently insert it into the socket. Step 2, secure the CPU to the socket on the motherboard with the latch plate. Step 3, secure the latch plate by closing the load lever. Step 4, secure the load lever under the load lever retention tab. Step 5, apply thermal compound to the CPU. Step 6, align the heat sink and fan assembly retainers with the holes on the motherboard and place the assembly onto the CPU socket. Step 7, tighten the assembly retainers to secure the assembly in place.
